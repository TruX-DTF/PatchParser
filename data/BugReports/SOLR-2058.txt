                   <p>From Yonik Seeley &lt;yo...@lucidimagination.com&gt;<br> Perhaps fold it into the pf/pf2 syntax?</p> 
                   <p>pf=text^2 // current syntax... makes phrases with a boost of 2<br> pf=text~1^2 // proposed syntax... makes phrases with a slop of 1 and<br> a boost of 2</p> 
                   <p>That actually seems pretty natural given the lucene query syntax - an<br> actual boosted sloppy phrase query already looks like<br> <tt>text:"foo bar"~1^2</tt></p> 
                   <p>-Yonik</p>
