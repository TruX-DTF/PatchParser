               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The current GeoPointInBBoxQuery only supports bounding boxes that are within the standard -180:180 longitudinal bounds. While its perfectly fine to require users to split dateline crossing bounding boxes in two, GeoPointDistanceQuery should support distance queries that cross the dateline. Since morton encoding doesn't support unwinding this issue will add dateline crossing to GeoPointInBBoxQuery and GeoPointDistanceQuery classes.</p>
