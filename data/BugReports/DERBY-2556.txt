                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When using 'createFrom' or 'restoreFrom' in the JDBC url to restore a database from a backup image, a SecurityException is thrown even though the policyfile for codebase derby.jar is correctly configured (giving Derby access to the backup image).</p> 
                  <p>A few comments on this issue can be found here (and in subsequent comments): <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/DERBY-1001#action_12439811" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/DERBY-1001#action_12439811</a></p> 
                  <p>A workaround is wrapping the connection call in doPrivileged at the "application-level code", or granting the required permissions to the application codebase as well.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
