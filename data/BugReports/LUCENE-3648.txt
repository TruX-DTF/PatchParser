               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Since we moved the bulk reading into the codec ie. make all bulk reading codec private in <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-3584" title="bulk postings should be codec private" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-3584"><del>LUCENE-3584</del></a> we have seen some performance <a href="http://people.apache.org/~mikemccand/lucenebench/Term.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">regression</a> on different CPUs. I tried to optimize the implementation to make it more eligible for runtime optimizations, tried to make loops JIT friendly by moving out branches where I can, minimize member access in all loops, use final members where possible and specialize the two common cases With &amp; Without LiveDocs.</p> 
                  <p>I will attache a patch and my benchmark results in a minute.</p>
