               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Discovered by Mamta A. Satoor in <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/DERBY-1062" title="Change the internal implemetation of SYSCS_INPLACE_COMPRESS_TABLE to share existing alter table" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="DERBY-1062"><del>DERBY-1062</del></a>.</p> 
                  <p>SYSCS_BULK_INSERT builds an insert statement in which it doesn't quote the schema name or the table name. It also takes string parameters that are inserted into the statement text with single quotes around them, but that won't work if those strings contain single quote characters.</p>
