               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The facet module today requires the app to compute the hierarchy<br> at index time, eg a timestamp field might use a year/month/day<br> hierarchy.</p> 
                  <p>While this gives great performance, since it minimizes the search-time<br> computation, sometimes it's unfortunately useful/necessary to do things entirely at<br> search time, like Solr does.</p> 
                  <p>E.g. I'm playing with a prototype Lucene search for Jira issues<br> and I'd like to add a drill down+sideways for "Updated in past day,<br> 2 days, week, month" etc. But because time is constantly advancing,<br> doing this at index time is a not easy ...</p>
