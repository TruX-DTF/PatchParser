               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Currently when calling .assign() on a SASV with another vector and a custom function, it will iterate through it and assign every single entry while also referring it by index.</p> 
                  <p>This makes the process <b>hugely</b> expensive. (on a run of BallKMeans on the 20 newsgroups data set, profiling reveals that 92% of the runtime was spent updating assigning the vectors).</p> 
                  <p>Here's a prototype patch:<br> <a href="https://github.com/dfilimon/mahout/commit/63998d82bb750150a6ae09052dadf6c326c62d3d" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">https://github.com/dfilimon/mahout/commit/63998d82bb750150a6ae09052dadf6c326c62d3d</a></p>
