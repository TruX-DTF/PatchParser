                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>With the latest EA build of JDK 8 (build 1.8.0-ea-b89), I see many failures in suites.All. For example:</p> 
                  <p>1) testStartNetworkServerFalse(org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.derbynet.DerbyNetAutoStartTest)java.security.AccessControlException: access denied ("java.sql.SQLPermission" "deregisterDriver")<br> at java.security.AccessControlContext.checkPermission(AccessControlContext.java:364)<br> at java.security.AccessController.checkPermission(AccessController.java:562)<br> at java.lang.SecurityManager.checkPermission(SecurityManager.java:549)<br> at java.sql.DriverManager.deregisterDriver(DriverManager.java:399)<br> at org.apache.derby.jdbc.AutoloadedDriver.unregisterDriverModule(AutoloadedDriver.java:263)<br> at org.apache.derby.jdbc.Driver20.stop(Driver20.java:105)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.monitor.TopService.stop(TopService.java:443)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.monitor.TopService.shutdown(TopService.java:394)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.monitor.BaseMonitor.shutdown(BaseMonitor.java:227)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.monitor.FileMonitor.shutdown(FileMonitor.java:44)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.monitor.BaseMonitor.shutdown(BaseMonitor.java:197)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.monitor.FileMonitor.shutdown(FileMonitor.java:44)<br> at org.apache.derby.jdbc.InternalDriver.connect(InternalDriver.java:255)<br> at org.apache.derby.jdbc.Driver20.connect(Driver20.java:246)<br> at org.apache.derby.jdbc.AutoloadedDriver.connect(AutoloadedDriver.java:145)<br> at java.sql.DriverManager.getConnection(DriverManager.java:661)<br> at java.sql.DriverManager.getConnection(DriverManager.java:208)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.DriverManagerConnector.getConnectionByAttributes(DriverManagerConnector.java:204)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.DriverManagerConnector.shutEngine(DriverManagerConnector.java:171)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.TestConfiguration.shutdownEngine(TestConfiguration.java:1822)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.derbynet.DerbyNetAutoStartTest.setUp(DerbyNetAutoStartTest.java:82)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.BaseTestCase.runBare(BaseTestCase.java:117)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.BaseJDBCTestCase.runBareOverridable(BaseJDBCTestCase.java:439)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.BaseJDBCTestCase.runBare(BaseJDBCTestCase.java:456)</p> 
                  <p>What's new in EA build 89 is that DriverManager.deregisterDriver() now requires an SQLPermission when running under a security manager. Most of suites.All runs under a security manager, and Derby's engine shutdown code calls deregisterDriver(), so this problem probably affects all tests that shut down the engine.</p>
