                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When indexing large (1 megabyte) documents I get a lot of exceptions with stack traces like the below. It happens both in the Solr 1.3 release and in the July 9 1.4 nightly. I believe this to NOT be the same issue as <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/SOLR-42" title="Highlighting problems with HTMLStripWhitespaceTokenizerFactory" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="SOLR-42"><del>SOLR-42</del></a>. I found some further discussion on solr-user: <a href="http://www.nabble.com/IOException:-Mark-invalid-while-analyzing-HTML-td17052153.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://www.nabble.com/IOException:-Mark-invalid-while-analyzing-HTML-td17052153.html</a> </p> 
                  <p>In that discussion, Grant asked the original poster to open a Jira issue, but I didn't see one so I'm opening one; please feel free to merge or close if it's redundant. </p> 
                  <p>My stack trace follows.</p>
