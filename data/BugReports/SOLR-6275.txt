               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Currently, <tt>QTime</tt> uses <tt>currentTimeMillis</tt> instead of <tt>nano Time</tt> and hence is not suitable for time measurements. Further, it is really started after all the dispatch logic in <tt>SolrDispatchFilter</tt> (same with the top level timing reported by <tt>debug=timing</tt>) which may or may not be expensive, and hence may not fully represent the time taken by the search. This is to remedy both cases.</p>
