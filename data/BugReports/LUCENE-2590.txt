                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The ability to gather more details than just the score, of how a given<br> doc matches the current query, has come up a number of times on the<br> user's lists. (most recently in the thread "Query Match Count" by<br> Ryan McV on java-user).</p> 
                  <p>EG if you have a simple TermQuery "foo", on each hit you'd like to<br> know how many times "foo" occurred in that doc; or a BooleanQuery +foo<br> +bar, being able to separately see the freq of foo and bar for the<br> current hit.</p> 
                  <p>Lucene doesn't make this possible today, which is a shame because<br> Lucene in fact does compute exactly this information; it's just not<br> accessible from the Collector.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
