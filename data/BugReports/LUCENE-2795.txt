               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Today DirectIOLinuxDir is tricky/dangerous to use, because you only want to use it for indexWriter and not IndexReader (searching). It's a trap.</p> 
                  <p>But, once we do <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-2793" title="Directory createOutput and openInput should take an IOContext" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-2793"><del>LUCENE-2793</del></a>, we can make it fully general purpose because then a single native Dir impl can be used.</p> 
                  <p>I'd also like to make it generic to other Unices, if we can, so that it becomes UnixDirectory.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
