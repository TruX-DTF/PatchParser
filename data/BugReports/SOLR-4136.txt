                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>SolrCloud does not work properly with non-trivial values for "hostContext" (ie: the servlet context path). In particular...</p> 
                  <ul> 
                   <li>Using a hostContext containing a "/" (ie: a servlet context with a subdir path, semi-common among people who organize webapps hierarchically for lod blanacer rules) is explicitly forbidden in ZkController because of how the hostContext is used to build a ZK nodeName</li> 
                   <li>Using a hostContext containing a "_" causes problems in OverseerCollectionProcessor where it assumes all "_" characters should be converted to "/" to reconstitute a URL from nodeName (NOTE: this code specifically has a TODO to fix this, and then has a subsequent TODO about assuming "http://" labeled "this sucks")</li> 
                  </ul> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
