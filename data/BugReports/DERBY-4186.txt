                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Occasionally I see this error in ReplicationRun_Local_3_p3:</p> 
                  <p>1) testReplication_Local_3_p3_StateNegativeTests(org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.replicationTests.ReplicationRun_Local_3_p3)junit.framework.AssertionFailedError: Expected SQLState'08004', but got connection!<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.replicationTests.ReplicationRun.waitForSQLState(ReplicationRun.java:332)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.replicationTests.ReplicationRun_Local_3_p3.testReplication_Local_3_p3_StateNegativeTests(ReplicationRun_Local_3_p3.java:170)<br> at sun.reflect.NativeMethodAccessorImpl.invoke0(Native Method)<br> at sun.reflect.NativeMethodAccessorImpl.invoke(NativeMethodAccessorImpl.java:39)<br> at sun.reflect.DelegatingMethodAccessorImpl.invoke(DelegatingMethodAccessorImpl.java:25)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.BaseTestCase.runBare(BaseTestCase.java:105)<br> at junit.extensions.TestDecorator.basicRun(TestDecorator.java:24)<br> at junit.extensions.TestSetup$1.protect(TestSetup.java:21)<br> at junit.extensions.TestSetup.run(TestSetup.java:25)</p> 
                  <p>In the code, after a stopMaster is given to the master (should lead to fail-over),<br> the tests expects to see CANNOT_CONNECT_TO_DB_IN_SLAVE_MODE (08004.C.7), which will only succeed if<br> the tests gets to try to connect before the failover has started. This seems wrong. If the failover has completed, it should expect a successful<br> connect (which boots the database, btw, since its shut down after auccessful failover).</p> 
                  <p>Quote from code:</p> 
                  <p>waitForSQLState("08004", 100L, 20, // 08004.C.7 - CANNOT_CONNECT_TO_DB_IN_SLAVE_MODE<br> slaveDatabasePath + FS + slaveDbSubPath + FS + replicatedDb,<br> slaveServerHost, slaveServerPort); // _failOver above fails...</p> 
                  <p>There is a race between the failover on the slave and the test here I think.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
