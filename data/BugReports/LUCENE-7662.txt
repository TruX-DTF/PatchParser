               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Similar to what we did in <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-7592" title="EOFException while opening index should be rethrown as CorruptIndexException" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-7592"><del>LUCENE-7592</del></a> for EOF, we should catch missing files and rethrow those as CorruptIndexException.</p> 
                  <p>If a particular codec can handle missing files, it should be proactive check for those optional files and not throw anything, so I think we can safely do this at SegmentReader or SegmentCoreReaders level.</p> 
                  <p>Stack trace copied from <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/SOLR-10006" title="Cannot do a full sync (fetchindex) if the replica can't open a searcher" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="SOLR-10006">SOLR-10006</a>:</p>
