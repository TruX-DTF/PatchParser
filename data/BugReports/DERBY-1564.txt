                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The wisconsin test failed on some Solaris (sparc) platforms during testing of the 10.2.0.4 snapshot, in either the DerbyNet or DerbyNetClient framework. </p> 
                  <p>No output in the outfile. On some platforms the DerbyNet.err file has one message:</p> 
                  <p>Exception in thread "Thread-2" java.lang.OutOfMemoryError: Java heap space</p> 
                  <p>On some platforms the OutOfMemoryError is also (or instead) reported in the derby.log file.<br> All test machines had 2 CPUs and 2 GB of RAM.</p> 
                  <p>Here is a list of platforms where it failed:</p> 
                  <p>Java 6 (Mustang, build 91) :<br> --------------------------------------------------</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 10 (sparc)<br> derbyall/derbynetmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 8 (sparcN-2)<br> derbyall/derbynetmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 10, local zone (sparc_zone1)<br> derbyall/derbynetmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 10, local zone (sparc_zone3)<br> derbynetclientmats/derbynetmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 10, global zone (zones)<br> derbynetmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java</p> 
                  <p>Java 5 (Sun's HotSpot VM, v1.5.0):<br> ---------------------------------------------------------------</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 9 (sparcN-1) <br> derbyall/derbynetclientmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java</p> 
                  <p>Solaris 8 (sparcN-2)<br> derbyall/derbynetmats/derbynetmats.fail:lang/wisconsin.java </p> 
                  <p>See <a href="http://www.nabble.com/10.2.0.4-Test-results-p5485739.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://www.nabble.com/10.2.0.4-Test-results-p5485739.html</a> for details.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
