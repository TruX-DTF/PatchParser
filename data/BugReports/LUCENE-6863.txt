               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>For both NUMERIC fields and ordinals of SORTED fields, we store data in a dense way. As a consequence, if you have only 1000 documents out of 1B that have a value, and 8 bits are required to store those 1000 numbers, we will not require 1KB of storage, but 1GB.</p> 
                  <p>I suspect this mostly happens in abuse cases, but still it's a pity that we explode storage requirements. We could try to detect sparsity and compress accordingly.</p>
