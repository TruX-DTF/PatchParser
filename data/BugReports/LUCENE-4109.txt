                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Hi,</p> 
                  <p>I just found another bug in the flexible query parser (together with Robert Muir, yay!).</p> 
                  <p>The following query string works in the standard query parser:</p> 
                  <div class="preformatted panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="preformattedContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre>(field:[1 TO *] AND field:[* TO 2]) AND field2:z
</pre> 
                   </div>
                  </div> 
                  <p>yields</p> 
                  <div class="preformatted panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="preformattedContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre>+(+field:[1 TO *] +field:[* TO 2]) +field2:z
</pre> 
                   </div>
                  </div> 
                  <p>The flexible query parser though yields:</p> 
                  <div class="preformatted panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="preformattedContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre>+(field:[1 TO *] field:[* TO 2]) +field2:z
</pre> 
                   </div>
                  </div> 
                  <p>Test patch is attached (from Robert actually).</p> 
                  <p>I don't know if it affects earlier versions than 3.5.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
