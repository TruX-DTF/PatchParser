                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Spark bindings for Mahout DRM. <br> DRM DSL. </p> 
                  <p>Disclaimer. This will all be experimental at this point.</p> 
                  <p>The idea is to wrap DRM by Spark RDD with support of some basic functionality, perhaps some humble beginning of Cost-based optimizer </p> 
                  <p>(0) Spark serialization support for Vector, Matrix <br> (1) Bagel transposition <br> (2) slim X'X<br> (2a) not-so-slim X'X<br> (3) blockify() (compose RDD containing vertical blocks of original input)<br> (4) read/write Mahout DRM off HDFS<br> (5) A'B</p>
