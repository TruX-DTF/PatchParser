                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p><tt>TokenizerChain</tt> created using 2-arg constructor has <tt>null</tt> in <tt>charFilters</tt>, so <tt>LukeRequestHandler</tt> throws NPE on iterating it.</p> 
                  <p><tt>TokenizerChain</tt> constructor's should be hardened to do explicit null checks, throwing early NPE where appropriate (tokenizer factory), or initializing internal arrays to have 0 length when optional (factories for char filters and token filters)</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
