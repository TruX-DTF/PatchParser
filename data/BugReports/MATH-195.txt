               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I think there are some inconsistencies in the hierarchy of exceptions used by the Commons Math library which could easily be fixed:</p> 
                  <ul class="alternate" type="square"> 
                   <li>org.apache.commons.math.linear.MatrixIndexException should really be a subclass of<br> java.lang.IndexOutOfBoundsException.</li> 
                  </ul> 
                  <ul class="alternate" type="square"> 
                   <li>Either org.apache.commons.math.linear.InvalidMatrixException should be checked or<br> org.apache.commons.math.random.NotPositiveDefiniteMatrixException and<br> org.apache.commons.math.geometry.NotARotationMatrixException should be unchecked, but in any case the latter two<br> should be subclasses of the former. An unchecked InvalidMatrixException should probably be a subclass of<br> java.lang.ArithmeticException while a checked InvalidMatrixException should be a subclass of<br> org.apache.commons.math.MathException.</li> 
                  </ul> 
                 </div>
