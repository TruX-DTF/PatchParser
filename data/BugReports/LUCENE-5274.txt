                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I've been messing around with the FastVectorHighlighter and it looks like I can teach it to highlight matches on "child fields". Like this query:<br> foo:scissors foo_exact:running<br> would highlight foo like this:<br> &lt;em&gt;running&lt;/em&gt; with &lt;em&gt;scissors&lt;/em&gt;</p> 
                  <p>Where foo is stored WITH_POSITIONS_OFFSETS and foo_plain is an unstored copy of foo a different analyzer and its own WITH_POSITIONS_OFFSETS.</p> 
                  <p>This would make queries that perform weighted matches against different analyzers much more convenient to highlight.</p> 
                  <p>I have working code and test cases but they are hacked into Elasticsearch. I'd love to Lucene-ify if you'll take them.</p> 
                 </div>
