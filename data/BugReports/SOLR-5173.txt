                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Chris Collins <a href="http://markmail.org/message/evhpcougs5ppafjk" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">reported on solr-user</a> that solr-core 4.4 has dependencies on hadoop, and indirectly on jetty 6.</p> 
                  <p>As a workaround for Maven dependencies, the indirect jetty 6 dependency/ies can be excluded.</p> 
                  <p>The Maven configuration should exclude any compile-time (and also run-time) dependencies that are not Ant/Ivy compile- and run-time dependencies, including jetty 6.</p> 
                 </div>
